JobScheduler and JADE repositories

This is a short introduction about:

  • what projects the repositories are about
  • what repositories are required in order to build from the sources
  • where the build instructions are located

Projects

The repositories correspond to the projects JobScheduler and JADE. JobScheduler and JADE are the two Open Source products the company offers.

Required Repositories

In our GitHub site twelve repositories are available. Three of them have to be cloned when building the products from the sources:

  • scheduler-engine-doc
  • scheduler-engine
  • all
The remaining repositories are submodules of the repository all that are published for reference purposes when building the products.
